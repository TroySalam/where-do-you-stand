How It Works

The Grid

Your answers map to two axes — Progressive ↔ Traditional (left-right) and More Government ↔ Less Government (up-down). Your final position is a single dot on this grid.

Modes

Teen uses everyday scenarios. Adult covers real-world policy. Academic brings in political theory. Economist uses complex, data-driven dilemmas.

Scoring

Each answer nudges your dot in a direction. More questions = more accurate placement. We also track 12 separate topic scores behind the scenes so you can see where you lean on each issue.

Privacy

Nothing is stored, sent, or tracked. Everything runs in your browser. Close the tab and it's gone.
